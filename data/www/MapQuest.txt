MapQuest (stylized as mapquest) is an American free online web mapping service owned by Oath Inc. The company was founded in 1967 as Cartographic Services, a division of R.R. Donnelley & Sons in Chicago, Illinois, United States. It moved to Lancaster, Pennsylvania in 1969. When it became an independent company in 1994, it was renamed GeoSystems Global Corporation. MapQuest was acquired in 2000 by America Online, Inc. Company headquarters are in Denver, Colorado. As of May 2015, it had the second-highest share of the online mapping market in the United States, second only to Google Maps.
