HelloGiggles.com is an entertainment and lifestyle website launched in May 2011. It was founded by actress/musician Zooey Deschanel, producer Sophia Rivka Rossi and writer Molly McAleer. The website is geared toward women, and covers topics in popular culture, love, friendship, female empowerment, careers, style, food and daily news. HelloGiggles.com is marketed as a positive online community by its users with a strict "no gossip" policy. Reader contributions are permitted, and many are published every day.
