Pauline C. Krikke (born 9 May 1961 in Sneek) is a Dutch politician for the People's Party for Freedom and Democracy (VVD). From 3 September 2001 till 1 July 2013, she was mayor of the municipality of Arnhem. Before that, she was an Alderman in the municipality of Amsterdam. Between 9 June 2015 and 16 March 2017 she was a member of the Senate. She has been mayor of The Hague since 17 March 2017.
