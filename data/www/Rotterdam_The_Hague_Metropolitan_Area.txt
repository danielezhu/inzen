The Rotterdam The Hague Metropolitan Area (Dutch: Metropoolregio Rotterdam-Den Haag) is a cooperation between the cities of Rotterdam and The Hague and 21 other municipalities. It finds its legal basis in law. It was founded in 2014. The area has a population of over 2 million across 1,130 km2 (440 sq mi). The largest European port and many international organizations reside within its borders. The area is also part of the larger urban area called Randstad. Air traffic is supported by Rotterdam-The Hague Airport offering services to a number of European cities.
RandstadRail offers light rail public transport in the area.
