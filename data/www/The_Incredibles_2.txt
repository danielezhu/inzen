The Incredibles 2 is an upcoming 2018 American 3D computer-animated superhero film, the sequel to The Incredibles (2004). It is in production by Pixar Animation Studios, and will be released by Walt Disney Pictures. It is being written and directed by Brad Bird, the director of the first film. The movie is scheduled to be released on June 15, 2018 and will be given an IMAX release.
