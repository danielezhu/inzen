Max Lousada is Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Warner Music UK. He has signed and worked with a number of artists, including Ed Sheeran. His influence on British popular culture due to his position at Warner Music UK and as Chairman of the Brits Committee saw him appear on Debrett's 2017 list of the most influential people in the UK.
Max Lousada will become CEO, Recorded Music for Warner Music Group from 1 October 2017.
