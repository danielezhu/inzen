Randy Napoleon (born 30 May 1978) is an American jazz guitarist, composer, and arranger who is a member of the Freddy Cole Quartet and the leader of a sextet, a quartet and a trio. He is Assistant Professor of Jazz Guitar at Michigan State University in the College of Music.
