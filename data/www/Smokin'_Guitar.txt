Smokin' Guitar is the second live album by American jazz guitarist Wes Montgomery, released in 1965. This is also Montgomery's fourteenth overall album.
On almost all tracks, there is the same line-up that appeared on Smokin' at the Half Note album, but at a later date. It was recorded live at the Half Note Club, New York City, in 1965, September 24 (3 & 4); November 12 (1 & 2); November 19 (5 & 6)
