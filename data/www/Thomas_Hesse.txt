Thomas Hesse is a senior media and digital media executive and entrepreneur. He most recently was the CEO of online auction house Paddle8 Auctionata. He is a Senior Adviser to CVC Capital Partners and Founder of Consonance Investments LLC, an angel/ VC fund and a venture partner of Foundersguild. He was previously President of Corporate Development and New Businesses as well as Member of the Executive Board of Bertelsmann, and before that Sony Music Entertainment's President of Global Digital Business & US Sales.
