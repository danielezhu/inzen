The Big Game is an American college football rivalry game played by the California Golden Bears football team of the University of California, Berkeley and Stanford Cardinal football team of Stanford University. First played 125 years ago in 1892, it is one of the oldest college rivalries in the United States. Stanford leads the series 62-46-11. The game is typically played in late November or early December, and its location alternates between the two universities every year.
