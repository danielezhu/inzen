RYOT /ˈrīət/ (or, riot) is an immersive media company founded in 2012 by Bryn Mooser, David Darg, Molly Swenson and Martha Rogers, based in Los Angeles. It specializes in film production, television production, financing, commercial production, virtual reality, augmented reality. 
In April 2016, RYOT was acquired by The Huffington Post / AOL in the first VR content company exit in history.
