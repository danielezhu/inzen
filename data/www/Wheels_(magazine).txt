Wheels is an Australian automotive magazine owned by Bauer Media Group. The publication is well-renowned by Australian car enthusiasts. Its main competitor within the Australian car magazine market is Motor, though Wheels and Motor are stablemates in the Bauer Media Group catalogue and are aimed at slightly different readers as Motor puts attention on performance cars. Wheels magazine is sold in New Zealand and Australia only and can be found at stores such as newsagents. The magazine is published monthly by Bauer Media Pty Ltd. and has its headquarters in Melbourne.
Its current editor is Alex Inwood, who has held the position as of the December issue.
