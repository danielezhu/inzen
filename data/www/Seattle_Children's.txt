Seattle Children's, formerly Children's Hospital and Regional Medical Center, formerly Children's Orthopedic Hospital, is a children's hospital in the Laurelhurst neighborhood of Seattle, Washington. In 2015, it was ranked as the 6th best children's hospital in America by U.S. News and World Report and was ranked #3 in nephrology, #5 in cancer, #5 in neonatology, #8 in gastroenterology and GI surgery, #9 in pulmonology and #10 in neurology and neurosurgery.
