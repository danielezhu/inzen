Segbroek (Dutch pronunciation: [ˈsɛxbruk]) is one of eight districts of The Hague in the Netherlands. The area was annexed by the city in 1988. It has a total area of 7.19 square kilometres (2.78 sq mi) and a population of 60,054 as of January 2013. Segbroek is divided into five neighbourhoods: Bomen- en Bloemenbuurt, Regentessekwartier, Valkenboskwartier, Vogelwijk and Vruchtenbuurt.
