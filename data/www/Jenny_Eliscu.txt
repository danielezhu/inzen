Jenny Eliscu is a radio host for Sirius XM and a contributing editor for Rolling Stone magazine. She also had a recurring presence on the TV program I'm from Rolling Stone, and has been on other music programs, including Behind the Music. She has written a book (Schools That Rock: The Rolling Stone College Guide ISBN 1-932958-53-3).
She wrote the liner notes for Britney Spears' greatest hits album Greatest Hits: My Prerogative.
