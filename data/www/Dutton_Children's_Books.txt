Dutton Children's Books is a US publisher, a division of the Penguin Group, the children's book imprint associated with the Dutton adult division. They were previously an imprint of E.P. Dutton prior to 1986. The Dutton children's imprint, as a division of E.P. Dutton, has been publishing since 1852. They are the original US publishers of the Winnie-the-Pooh books by A.A. Milne, among others.
